Overview

What type of bodily structure are tonsils?

They are secondary lymphatic organs found at the back of the mouth on either side of the epiglottis

What is tonsilitis?

Inflammation of the tonsils.

What causes tonsilitis?

It is usually caused by a viral infection - some cases are caused by bacterial infections.

What is streptococcus pyogenes?

An alternative name for the pathogenic Streptococcus A bacteria which cause infections such as strep throat, sepsis, tonsilitis and scarlet fever

How can clostridium difficile infections arise?

  • C. difficile bacteria typically live in the gastrointestinal tract
  • During the use of (specifically broad-spectrum) antibiotics, these C. difficile bacteria can become resistant and have advantages over others (strep A doesn’t become resistant)
  • This causes them to replicate faster and increase in number, and infection is spread across the body.
